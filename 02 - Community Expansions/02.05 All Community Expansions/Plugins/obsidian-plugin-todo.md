# Obsidian TODO | Text-based GTD

Plugin ID: `obsidian-plugin-todo`

Text-based GTD in Obsidian. Collects all outstanding TODOs from your vault and presents them in lists Today, Scheduled, Inbox and Someday/Maybe.
